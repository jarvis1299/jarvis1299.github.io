DESCRIPTION

SECRET NOURISHING OIL is packed full of antioxidants and moisturizers including Jojoba Oil, Rosehip Oil, Argan Oil, Vitamin C and Vitamin E. This powerhouse of ingredients leaves skin feeling intensely nourished and looking radiant.

ACTIVE INGREDIENTS 

Jojoba Oil
This oil has been shown to enhance skin’s restorative properties and is a rich source of fatty acids that can also provide skin-soothing benefits.

Rosehip Oil

Rosehip oil is quick to absorb, replenishing moisture and creating a protective barrier on the skin to help prevent dehydration.


Vitamin C
Vitamin C plays a role in collagen syntheses, helps to even your skin tone, revitalize skin's surface and can significantly improve hydration.


Vitamin E
Vitamin E is an antioxidant that helps block free radicals that can cause damage to the skin.

Argan Oil
Argan oil contains antioxidant and essential fatty acids that are intensely moisturizing without feeling greasy and clogging pores. 

DIMISH THE APPEARANCE OF FINE LINES & WRINKLES

Secret Nourishing Oil can help reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles with its powerhouse of ingredients, including rose oil, jojoba seed, argan, vitamin C & E all rich in antioxidants and fatty acids making it our best facial oil to date.

MULTIPURPOSE, EASILY ABSORBED, VELVETY FINISH 

A lot of people think facial oils are, well oily! Our facial oil serum is light weight, non-greasy and leaves no residue. Whilst this was developed for skincare, it can be used as a treatment for the body, frizzy hair, for acne and by men and women alike.

LOOK RADIANT AND STAY SUPER HYDRATED

Oils deliver a moisture boost to help balance, replenish and restore your skin and help it resist moisture loss to stay hydrated all day long. You’ll be positively glowing!

ORGANIC INGREDIENTS AND MADE IN THE USA

Secret Nourishing Oil contains certified organic ingredients and is manufactured in the USA at a GMP certified and FDA regulated facility and is always cruelty-free.

 

DIRECTIONS

Gently massage 1 – 2 drops of oil into face and neck each morning and evening. Follow with moisturizer.

Not to be used during pregnancy and nursing.

 

INGREDIENTS

Caprylic Capric Triglycerides, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil*, Rosa Canina (Rosehip) Seed Oil*, Tocopherol, Tetrahexyldecyl Ascorbate, Argania Spinosa (Argan) Kernel Oil.
